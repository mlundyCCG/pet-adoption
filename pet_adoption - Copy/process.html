Starting the Process

Ready to bring in your newest addition to the family? We'd love to help you! However, our top priority is ensuring that these pets go to a loving and safe home. The approval process usually takes anywhere from 5-8 weeks.

That being said, below you can learn more about the process involved with the adoption below. If you think you're ready, you can gho straight to the application form here: Link form here

Process Overview

1. The Application

Start by filling out the application found below. We will review it and get back to you within 2-5 business days.

Link form here

2. The Interview

Upon approval of your application, we will send you an email asking you to schedule a brief phone interview.

The purpose of this interview will be to get to know you and ask a few introductory questions.

3. The Home Visit

We will perform a home visit. This is a mandatory step in the process to ensure the safty of the pets.

We do not go through personal spaces or belongings. It is a check to make sure that the living space is not cluttered, fences are up to par, and a few other variables.

4. The Pet Introduction!

The moment you have been waiting for is finally here. We will schedule a time for one of our trained staff to bring your new pet over for an introduction!

Please note: This visit will not be too long. Each visit at this stage will last between 30 minutes to an hour. If you have other pets, we will slowly introduce your new pets so that everything goes smoothly.

We will repeat this step a few times if needed. Generally 1-2 visits is enough.

6. The New Addition

The wait is over! At this point, all approvals will be cleared and you can bring your new pet home.

Your pet can be picked up at our facility, or we can drop them off for you.
